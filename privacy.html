Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This page explains what happens to your information when you use jeffreypotts.ca. The short version: this is a small author site, it collects the minimum needed to send you what you asked for, and nothing is sold or shared with advertisers.

What I collect

  • Newsletter signup: the name and email address you enter in the signup form.
  • Contact form: the name, email address, and message you enter.

I don't use cookies, analytics, or advertising trackers on this site.

How it's used

Newsletter signups are stored in a database so I can send early chapters and occasional updates. Contact form messages are emailed to me directly and are not stored separately — they're not kept anywhere beyond my inbox.

Who else sees it

This site runs on Cloudflare, which hosts the site and stores the subscriber list. Emails (welcome messages and contact form notifications) are sent through Resend, an email delivery service. Both process data only as needed to run this site — neither is used for advertising, and your information isn't sold or shared with anyone else.

Your choices

Every newsletter email includes an unsubscribe option. If you'd like your information removed sooner, or have any question about what's stored, just email me at me@jeffreypotts.ca and I'll take care of it.

Changes

If this policy changes, the "last updated" date above will change with it.
